Bulbasaur

Who?

Bulbasaur, known as Fushigidane in Japan, is the first Pokémon in Nintendo and Game Freak's Pokémon franchise's monster dictonary, called a PokéDex. Designed by Atsuko Nishida, Bulbasaur debuted in Pokémon Red and Green as a Starter Pokémon.

What are his supernatural abilities?

Bulbasaur's are known to be strong Pokemon. From a young age, they are capable of utilitizing basic attacks, such as tackle and take down. As they grow and develop, they may learn a more powerful version of these aforementioned powers, known as double-edge.
