Petra

The Pink City

description

An archaeological and historical city located in the Ma'an Governorate in the south of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan.
It is famous for its rock-carved architecture and ancient aqueduct system

It was called

  • "سلع"
  • "المدينة الوردية"

Petra was established roughly in 312 BC as the capital of the Nabataean kingdom.
It assumed a prominent position for many years, as it was located on:

  • the Silk Road
  • the middle of the Mesopotamian civilizations
    1. Palestine
    2. Egypt
